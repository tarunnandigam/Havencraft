Returns Policy

30-Day Return Policy

We want you to love your handmade purchase! If you're not completely satisfied, you can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be in original, unused condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be included
  • Return must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom or personalized items
  • Perishable goods
  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
  • Intimate or sanitary goods

How to Return

  1. Contact our customer service team
  2. Receive return authorization and instructions
  3. Package item securely with original packaging
  4. Ship to provided return address
  5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange items for different sizes or colors when available. Exchange shipping costs apply unless the item was defective or incorrect.

Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged, please contact us immediately with photos. We'll provide a prepaid return label and full refund or replacement.
